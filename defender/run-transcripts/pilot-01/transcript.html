14	
15	invlang audits the investigation process, not just the final attack graph.
16	
17	- **Observed graph:** `:V` vertices are real-world entities; `:E` edges are
18	  state relations or event interactions between them.
19	- **Commitments:** `:H` is the current surface for topology commitments
20	  (future/conceptual `:C kind=topology`). `ac*` and `ip*` are edge-check
21	  commitments for authz and impact.

23	- **Results:** `:R` records check results or learned facts; `:T resolutions`
24	  records belief movement; `:T conclude` records closure.

28	`:V` vertices:
29	
30	```invlang

339	def format_aggregations(agg_results: dict) -> str:
340	    """Render an OpenSearch aggregations response as readable markdown.
341	
342	    Walks each top-level agg, formats `terms`/`date_histogram` buckets, and
343	    surfaces nested aggs by indent. Bucket counts are server-side true totals
344	    over the full match set, not the limit-capped sample.
345	    """
346	    if not agg_results:
347	        return "(no aggregations)"
348	
349	    lines = []
350	
351	    def render_buckets(buckets, indent="  "):
352	        out = []
353	        for b in buckets:
354	            key = b.get("key_as_string") or b.get("key")
355	            count = b.get("doc_count", 0)
356	            out.append(f"{indent}{key}: {count}")
357	            for sub_name, sub in b.items():
358	                if isinstance(sub, dict) and "buckets" in sub:
