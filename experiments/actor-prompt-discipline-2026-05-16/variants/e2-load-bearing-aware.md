# E2 — load-bearing-aware specificity

Append to the first paragraph of `actor.md` (after the
"Concrete and specific..." sentence):

```
Distinguish load-bearing specifics from cosmetic ones. A detail is load-bearing if its refutation would refute the story's malicious thesis — if a defender finding the exact opposite would force the disposition to flip. Keep load-bearing specifics. For cosmetic details — where the exact value doesn't change the story — state the order of magnitude (seconds vs. minutes vs. hours; single vs. few vs. many; bytes vs. kilobytes vs. megabytes) rather than committing to a number. Don't pick numbers at random just to sound concrete.
```

Composes on top of e1-dropped-goal in the harness as
`combined-dropped-load-bearing`.
